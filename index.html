Deep Technology Expertise


Natural Language Understanding

Our expertise in natural language ranges from Information Extraction from structured and unstructured data, Summarization, Text Classification, Sentiment Analysis, Named Entity Detection and Topic modeling.

One of real-world examples is Zemoso’s IP product app, Textractive, which summarizes phone conversations and meetings and extract action items and list them for follow up.

Some of the frameworks libraries we use extensively are: tensorflow, BERT, Spacy, scikit-learn, rasa, NLU.

Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence

Zemoso engineers work daily with speech-to-text detection, detecting the onset of disease, chatbots and conversational assistants filling in missing characters and predicting scanned document fills, data augmentation and generation, object detection in videos and images, plus anomaly detection.


We specialize in Deep Neural Network models — CNN, DNN, Bidirectional Encoder Representations from Transformers (BERT), Universal Sentence Encoder and survival regression models. We have an in-house domain-focused Speech-to Text engine built on DeepSpeech 2, which has been trained using 50 terabytes of audio files(40,000 hours).


Some of the frameworks and libraries we use are Tensorflow, scikit-learn, PyTorch, SparkML.
